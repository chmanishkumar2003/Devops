Quick Masala Oats: A Healthy Breakfast in 15 Minutes
Busy mornings often leave little time for preparing a healthy meal. If you're looking for something quick, nutritious, and flavorful, Masala Oats can be a perfect choice.

Masala Oats combines the goodness of oats with fresh vegetables and simple Indian spices. It is rich in fiber, helps keep you full for longer, and can be prepared in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients
1 cup oats

1 chopped onion

1 chopped tomato

½ cup mixed vegetables

1 green chili

Salt to taste

Turmeric powder

Mustard seeds

Method
Heat a pan and add mustard seeds.

Sauté onions, tomatoes, and vegetables.

Add turmeric and salt.

Pour water and bring it to a boil.

Add oats and cook for 5–7 minutes until soft.

Serve hot and enjoy a wholesome breakfast that keeps you energized throughout the day.

Healthy eating does not have to be complicated. With Masala Oats, you can prepare a delicious homemade meal quickly and start your day on the right note.
